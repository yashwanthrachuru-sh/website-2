Quick Administration Guide

Welcome to the moderator portal. From here, you have direct, local control over active resource states:

  • Users Database: Review the details of all registered users on this client.
  • Approvals Queue: Approve, reject, or revoke submissions. Approved materials immediately render within the student workspace catalog.
  • Audit Logs: View system event trails detailing moderation choices, logins, and logouts.
